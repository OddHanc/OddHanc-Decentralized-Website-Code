Hanc and the Last Person

One day, Hanc arrived in a city with only one inhabitant.

The man seemed happy.

Peaceful.

Content.

For years, Hanc visited him.

They spoke often.

The man never complained.

Never feared.

Never desired.

One evening, Hanc finally asked:

“How did you become so free?”

The man looked confused.

Then he replied:

“I am not free.”

“What do you mean?”

The man smiled.

“There is nobody left to imprison me.”
